Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Neon Overload handles information when you use the mobile application. Neon Overload is a mobile game with local progression, optional in-app purchases, optional rewarded ads, and optional advertising privacy consent flows.

Data collected

Neon Overload stores limited app data locally on your device so the game can function as expected. This local data may include progression, credits, unlocked upgrades, completed goals, language preference, and audio settings.

Neon Overload does not require an account for local progression and does not operate a first-party remote analytics backend.

If rewarded ads, consent flows, or in-app purchases are enabled in the published app, third-party providers such as Google advertising, consent, and store billing services may collect and process limited technical data needed to operate those services. This may include device or advertising identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, app interaction events, diagnostics, performance information, transaction status, and fraud-prevention or security signals.

How data is used

Local app data is used to save your game state and preferences on the device. This includes remembering your progression and gameplay-related settings between sessions.

If in-app purchases are offered, store providers may use relevant data to process purchases, restore entitlements, prevent fraud, and operate billing systems.

If rewarded ads or consent flows are offered, advertising and consent providers may use relevant data to deliver ads, record and apply privacy choices, measure service performance, prevent abuse, and support ad operations.

Third-party services

Neon Overload may use third-party mobile platform and monetization services, including app store billing services and Google ad and consent tooling, when those features are enabled in a given release.

When ads are active, the app may present a consent flow and privacy options form provided through Google's consent tooling so that users can review and manage advertising privacy choices where required.

Some of the limited technical data described above may also be shared with those third-party providers as part of ad delivery, billing, measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance operations.

Any data processing performed by third-party billing, advertising, or consent services is governed by those providers' own privacy terms and policies.

Data retention

Local game data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete this local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage through your device settings.

Debug logs generated inside the app remain local to the device unless you choose to export them manually for troubleshooting or support.

Security

Neon Overload is designed to keep core progression data on the device and to minimize first-party remote data handling. However, no device, network, or software environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's privacy

Neon Overload is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children through a first-party account system or direct submission forms. However, optional third-party monetization or platform services may process limited technical data when enabled in a release build.

Changes to this privacy policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features, data handling, or legal requirements change. Any update will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
