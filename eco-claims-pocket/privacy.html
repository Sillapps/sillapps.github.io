Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Eco Claims Pocket handles information when you use the mobile application. Eco Claims Pocket is a local-first reference app that helps users understand environmental marketing claims.

Data collected

Eco Claims Pocket does not collect, transmit, or share personal data from users.

The app stores limited app data locally on the device only so that core features can work as expected. This local data may include language preference, onboarding status, bookmarks, recent claim history, and the embedded glossary content and source references shipped with the app.

How data is used

Any local data stored by the app is used only to provide core app functionality on the device, such as remembering preferences, saved bookmarks, and recently viewed entries. This data is not used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or tracking.

Third-party services

Eco Claims Pocket does not use a remote backend, analytics SDK, ad SDK, or account system in its current version.

If you open a source link from within the app, that link is opened by your device browser or system handler. Any data processing performed by those third-party services is governed by their own privacy policies, not by the app itself.

Data retention

Because app data is stored locally on the device, it remains available until you remove it. You can delete this local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage through your device system settings.

Security

Eco Claims Pocket is designed to minimize data handling by keeping app data on the device and avoiding unnecessary remote services. However, no device or software environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's privacy

Eco Claims Pocket is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children. The app does not require account registration and does not provide forms for submitting personal information.

Changes to this privacy policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's data handling changes or if legal or product requirements evolve. Any update will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
